**App Description**

This macOS app automatically monitors the audio currently being played and quickly adjusts your selected output device to match the source audio’s native sample rate and bit depth when supported.

For example, when Apple Music plays a high-resolution track at **24-bit / 96 kHz**, but your DAC is currently set to **16-bit / 44.1 kHz**, the app detects the playback format and automatically switches the DAC output to **24-bit / 96 kHz**. This helps preserve audio quality by reducing unnecessary resampling and keeping the system output aligned with the original audio source.

The app is inspired by the idea and functionality of **LosslessSwitcher**, with a focus on fast sample-rate detection, automatic output switching, and improved listening accuracy for external DACs, Apple Music, and other high-resolution audio sources.

**Core functionality includes:**

* Detects the active audio source’s intended sample rate
* Automatically switches the selected macOS output device to the matching sample rate
* Supports common high-resolution formats such as 44.1 kHz, 48 kHz, 88.2 kHz, 96 kHz, 176.4 kHz, and 192 kHz when available
* Adjusts bit depth when supported by the connected DAC or audio interface
* Reduces manual setup in Audio MIDI Setup
* Helps avoid unwanted macOS audio resampling
* Runs quietly in the background for a seamless listening experience

**Suggested short version:**

A macOS utility that automatically detects the sample rate and bit depth of the audio being played and switches your DAC or output device to the matching format when supported, helping preserve lossless and hi-res audio playback without manual changes in Audio MIDI Setup.
